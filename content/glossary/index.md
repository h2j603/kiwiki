# 용어 사전 / Glossary

[웹키위바이러스](#웹키위바이러스--web-kiwi-virus) 관련 주요 용어를 정리합니다. / Key terms related to [Web Kiwi Viruses](#웹키위바이러스--web-kiwi-virus).

---

### 웹키위바이러스 / Web Kiwi Virus

웹을 통해 퍼지며 현실과 가상의 경계를 무너뜨리는 바이러스. 현재까지 [로직형](../taxonomy/index.md#유형별-분류), [감각형](../taxonomy/index.md#유형별-분류), [인지형](../taxonomy/index.md#유형별-분류), [환경형](../taxonomy/index.md#유형별-분류)의 4가지 유형이 확인되었다.

A virus that spreads through the web and collapses the boundary between the virtual and the real. Four types have been identified to date: [Logic](../taxonomy/index.md#classification-by-type), [Sensory](../taxonomy/index.md#classification-by-type), [Cognitive](../taxonomy/index.md#classification-by-type), and [Environmental](../taxonomy/index.md#classification-by-type).

ウェブを通じて広がり、現実と仮想の境界を崩壊させるウイルス。現在までに[ロジック型](../taxonomy/index.md#유형별-분류)、[感覚型](../taxonomy/index.md#유형별-분류)、[認知型](../taxonomy/index.md#유형별-분류)、[環境型](../taxonomy/index.md#유형별-분류)の4つのタイプが確認されている。
{: .ja}

---

### 경계 무너짐 / Boundary Collapse

웹 상의 현상이 현실에 영향을 미치기 시작하는 현상. 모든 웹키위바이러스의 핵심 메커니즘이며, 일반적으로 4~5단계에 걸쳐 진행된다. 각 바이러스마다 고유한 경계 무너짐 단계를 가진다.

The phenomenon where web-based effects begin to influence the physical world. This is the core mechanism of all Web Kiwi Viruses and generally progresses through 4-5 stages. Each virus has its own unique boundary collapse stages.

ウェブ上の現象が現実に影響を及ぼし始める現象。すべてのウェブキウイウイルスの中核メカニズムであり、一般的に4〜5段階を経て進行する。各ウイルスはそれぞれ固有の境界崩壊段階を持つ。
{: .ja}

- 예시 / Examples: [미러링크](../viruses/mirrorlink.md)의 반사면 회피, [글리치모스](../viruses/glitchmoss.md)의 공간 부식, [데드스크롤](../viruses/deadscroll.md)의 시간 빈틈

---

### 벡터 / Vector

바이러스가 전파되는 웹 경로. 현재 확인된 벡터는 [SNS](../taxonomy/index.md#전파-경로별-분류), [메신저](../taxonomy/index.md#전파-경로별-분류), [검색엔진](../taxonomy/index.md#전파-경로별-분류), [이메일](../taxonomy/index.md#전파-경로별-분류), [직접접근](../taxonomy/index.md#전파-경로별-분류)의 5가지.

The web pathway through which a virus spreads. Five vectors are currently identified: [Social Media](../taxonomy/index.md#classification-by-vector), [Messenger](../taxonomy/index.md#classification-by-vector), [Search Engine](../taxonomy/index.md#classification-by-vector), [Email](../taxonomy/index.md#classification-by-vector), and [Direct Access](../taxonomy/index.md#classification-by-vector).

ウイルスが伝播するウェブ経路。現在確認されているベクターは、[SNS](../taxonomy/index.md#전파-경로별-분류)、[メッセンジャー](../taxonomy/index.md#전파-경로별-분류)、[検索エンジン](../taxonomy/index.md#전파-경로별-분류)、[メール](../taxonomy/index.md#전파-경로별-분류)、[直接アクセス](../taxonomy/index.md#전파-경로별-분류)の5種類。
{: .ja}

### 발현 / Manifestation

바이러스가 현실에서 나타나는 구체적 증상. 유형에 따라 감각적([미러링크](../viruses/mirrorlink.md), [픽셀롯](../viruses/pixelrot.md)), 인지적([에코루프](../viruses/echoloop.md), [데드스크롤](../viruses/deadscroll.md)), 논리적([널위스퍼](../viruses/nullwhisper.md), [신택스레이스](../viruses/syntaxwraith.md)), 물리적([글리치모스](../viruses/glitchmoss.md), [캐시베인](../viruses/cachevein.md)) 발현으로 구분된다.

The specific symptoms through which a virus manifests in reality. Classified by type as: sensory ([MirrorLink](../viruses/mirrorlink.md), [PixelRot](../viruses/pixelrot.md)), cognitive ([EchoLoop](../viruses/echoloop.md), [DeadScroll](../viruses/deadscroll.md)), logical ([NullWhisper](../viruses/nullwhisper.md), [SyntaxWraith](../viruses/syntaxwraith.md)), or physical ([GlitchMoss](../viruses/glitchmoss.md), [CacheVein](../viruses/cachevein.md)).

ウイルスが現実で現れる具体的な症状。タイプに応じて、感覚的（[MirrorLink](../viruses/mirrorlink.md)、[PixelRot](../viruses/pixelrot.md)）、認知的（[EchoLoop](../viruses/echoloop.md)、[DeadScroll](../viruses/deadscroll.md)）、論理的（[NullWhisper](../viruses/nullwhisper.md)、[SyntaxWraith](../viruses/syntaxwraith.md)）、物理的（[GlitchMoss](../viruses/glitchmoss.md)、[CacheVein](../viruses/cachevein.md)）の発現に分類される。
{: .ja}

### 동시 감염 / Co-infection

둘 이상의 웹키위바이러스에 동시에 감염된 상태. 대부분의 경우 증상이 상승 작용을 일으켜 개별 감염보다 위험도가 크게 상승한다.

A state of being simultaneously infected by two or more Web Kiwi Viruses. In most cases, symptoms synergize, making co-infection significantly more dangerous than individual infections.

二つ以上のウェブキウイウイルスに同時に感染した状態。ほとんどの場合、症状が相乗効果を起こし、個別感染よりも危険度が大幅に上昇する。
{: .ja}

- 특히 위험한 조합 / Particularly dangerous combinations:
  - [글리치모스](../viruses/glitchmoss.md) + [캐시베인](../viruses/cachevein.md): 공간의 부식과 배선이 동시에 진행되어 건물 붕괴 위험 / Simultaneous spatial corrosion and wiring, risking building collapse
  - [에코루프](../viruses/echoloop.md) + [데드스크롤](../viruses/deadscroll.md): 반복과 시간 소멸의 이중 인지 장애 / Dual cognitive disorder of repetition and time erasure
  - [널위스퍼](../viruses/nullwhisper.md) + [신택스레이스](../viruses/syntaxwraith.md): 코드화된 사고가 자기 파괴적 결론만 출력 / Coded thought outputs only self-destructive conclusions

---

### 비가역적 변형 / Irreversible Transformation

경계 무너짐이 최종 단계에 도달하여 원래 상태로 복구할 수 없는 변화가 발생한 상태. 현재까지 [글리치모스](../viruses/glitchmoss.md)와 [캐시베인](../viruses/cachevein.md)에서만 확인됨.

A state where boundary collapse has reached its final stage, causing changes that cannot be restored to original condition. Currently confirmed only in [GlitchMoss](../viruses/glitchmoss.md) and [CacheVein](../viruses/cachevein.md).

境界崩壊が最終段階に達し、元の状態に復元できない変化が発生した状態。現在のところ[GlitchMoss](../viruses/glitchmoss.md)と[CacheVein](../viruses/cachevein.md)でのみ確認されている。
{: .ja}
